Derivative Works Guideline

Introduction

RISEUP INC (hereinafter to be referred to as "We", "Us", or "Our" as the case may be) has established these guidelines for the creation and use of derivative works regarding the contents of our "Project Nebula" so that fans may have the peace of mind to be able to enjoy our content in as many different forms as possible.

Derivative works followwing these guidelines do not have to request permission or contact us regarding the use of our content.

We would love for you to enjoy any deriverative works while following these guidelines and the code of conduct.

Applicable Cases

These guidelines apply to individual creators as well as groups that are not legally incorporated.

※If you represent a corporate body, we ask that you contact us individually.

Definition of Derivative Works

We consider works using our content as a base and expressing your creativity and ingenuity as derivative works. Please refrain from reuploading or reposting our content.

Please also be aware that our talents might post your derivative work on social media or use it as a video thumbnail.

Guidelines

Please follow the following rules when creating derivative works:

  • Please have any thrird party posting your derivative work aknowledge these derivative works.
  • You are allowed to sell/monetize your work, as long as it stays within the realm of amateur work. However, we do not allow any monetisation beyond the scope of cost recovery for individual creators.

Any derivative works containing any of the following items are not allowed:

  • Content that is contradictory to public order and morality, or exceeds what is socially acceptable
  • Content that is falsely represented as official, or can be misinterpreted or mistaken as official
  • Content that damages our image, or that of our talents or our content
  • Content that includes matters pertaining to any particular ideology, belief, religion, or politics
  • Content that damages a third party's image, or violates their rights
  • Other content that we deem unsuitable

Guidelines for Clips

For clips using our content, please make sure to follow the following rules:

  • Please make sure to always provide a link to the original source in the video description
  • Please refrain from creating clips of paid or membership-only content
  • As long as your clips follow the above guidelines, you may monetize your clips using YouTube's monetization system
